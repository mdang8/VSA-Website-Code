Question #4:

Subject: Microeconomics

Ok I know the following question is going to sound ridiculous, but just pretend we're in a world where this is completely possible. On average, a worker in Vietnam can grow a pound of rice in 20 minutes and a pound of noodles in 60 minutes. The average worker in China can grow a pound of rice in 50 minutes and a pound of noodles in 75 minutes. (a) Which country has the absolute advantage in noodles and why? (b) Which country has the comparative advantage in noodles and why? (c) Given the scenario where both countries specialize and trade with one another, which country will import noodles? Explain. (d) Both countries trade and the country importing noodles trades 2 pounds of rice for 1 pound of noodles. Explain why they will both benefit from this.
